Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

Welcome to MotelR. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This updated policy reflects our current practices and features.

Local Data Storage

MotelR operates entirely on your device. All data - including customer information, bookings, room details, and incident reports - is stored locally. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on external servers.

Security Features

We've implemented several security measures:

  • PIN protection for sensitive operations
  • Encrypted local backup system
  • Secure data restoration process
  • Protected incident reporting system

Photo & Document Storage

Images (such as driving license photos) and documents are stored locally on your device. We recommend:

  • Regular backups of your data
  • Secure device lock settings
  • Proper handling of sensitive documents

Customer Data Protection

MotelR allows you to store customer information including:

  • Names and contact details
  • Payment information
  • Identification documents
  • Incident reports
This information is secured through device-level encryption and PIN protection.

Analytics & Reports

All analytics and reports are generated locally on your device. Business metrics, occupancy rates, and financial data remain private and are not shared with any third parties.

Data Backup & Restore

The backup feature creates encrypted local copies of your data. When restoring data:

  • PIN verification is required
  • Data integrity is verified
  • Previous data is securely handled

User Responsibility

As the app operator, you are responsible for:

  • Maintaining device security
  • Protecting access credentials
  • Regular data backups
  • Proper handling of customer information
  • Compliance with local privacy laws

Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect app improvements and new features. Major changes will be notified through app updates.
